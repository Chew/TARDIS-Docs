Room Gallery

Here you can view all the rooms that are available in the TARDIS plugin.

Aquarium​

A room full of tanks of water, and used to store fish mobs.

Arboretum​

Defined as a botanical garden devoted to trees, an arboretum is a room full of trees and plants.

Apiary​

A room full of beehives, and used to store bee mobs.

Bamboo​

A room full of bamboo, and used to store panda mobs.

Bedroom​

A nice comfy room to sleep on!

Bird Cage​

A room full of bird cages, and used to store parrot mobs.

Chemistry Lab​

A room full of chemistry equipment. Learn more on the chemistry pages.

Empty​

A room with nothing in it. Useful for building your own rooms.

Exterior Rendering Room​

A room that shows the exterior of the TARDIS and the blocks surrounding it. See the scanner page.
