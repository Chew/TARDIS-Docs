Sparklers
===================

Sparklers work similar to how they do in real life. Right clicking one on air makes it sparkle for 30 seconds!

## Formula

`/tchemistry formula [colour]-sparkler`

Where `[colour]` is one of `blue`, `green`, `orange`, `purple`, or `red`
