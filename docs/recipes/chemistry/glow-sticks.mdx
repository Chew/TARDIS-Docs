Glow Sticks
===================

Glow sticks are cosmetic items that don't really do much on their own. They don't provide light, however holding one and right-clicking into the air will give it an enchanting effect.

## Formula

`/tchemistry formula [colour]-glow-stick`

Where `[colour]` is one of `white`, `orange`, `magenta`, `light-blue`, `yellow`, `lime`, `pink`, `light-gray`, `cyan`, `purple`, `blue`, `brown`, `green`, or `red`.
