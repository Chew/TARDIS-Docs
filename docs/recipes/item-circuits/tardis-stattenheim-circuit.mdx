TARDIS Stattenheim Circuit
===================

The Stattenheim Circuit is used in the crafting of a [Stattenhiem Remote](/recipes/items/stattenheim-remote). This circuit combines the [Materialization Circuit](/recipes/console-circuits/tardis-materialisation-circuit) and the [Locator Circuit](/recipes/item-circuits/tardis-locator-circuit) to allow the two to fit in the remote's casing.
